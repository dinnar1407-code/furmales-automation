## FAQ Sections

### Compress PDF

**Q: How to compress a PDF without losing quality?**
A: Use MiniAIPDF's AI compression mode. It automatically detects the best compression ratio while preserving visual quality. For best results, choose "Balanced" mode which reduces size by 50-80% with minimal quality loss.

**Q: What is the maximum PDF file size you can compress?**
A: Free users can compress PDFs up to 50MB. Premium users get up to 500MB per file. For larger files, consider splitting into smaller chunks first.

**Q: Does compression work on scanned PDFs?**
A: Yes, but scanned PDFs (image-based) typically compress less than text-based PDFs. For scanned documents, use OCR first to extract text, then compress.

---

### Merge PDF

**Q: How to merge multiple PDFs into one?**
A: Go to MiniAIPDF, select "Merge PDF", upload your files in order, and click "Merge". Download your combined PDF instantly.

**Q: Is there a limit on how many PDFs I can merge?**
A: Free users can merge up to 10 files at once. Premium users can merge unlimited files with total size up to 1GB.

**Q: Can I reorder pages after merging?**
A: Yes! Use our "Organize PDF" tool to reorder, delete, or rotate pages after merging.

---

### Split PDF

**Q: How to split a PDF by specific pages?**
A: Select "Split PDF", enter page ranges (e.g., "1-5, 10, 15-20"), and click "Split". Each range becomes a separate file.

**Q: Can I extract specific pages from a PDF?**
A: Yes, use "Extract Pages" to pull out only the pages you need. Enter page numbers or ranges to create a new PDF with just those pages.

---

### Protect PDF

**Q: How to password-protect a PDF?**
A: Use the "Protect PDF" tool, enter a strong password (12+ characters recommended), and download your encrypted PDF. AES-256 encryption keeps your document secure.

**Q: Can I remove password protection?**
A: Yes, if you have the original password. Use "Unlock PDF" tool to remove encryption.

**Q: What encryption does MiniAIPDF use?**
A: We use AES-256 bit encryption, the same standard used by banks and governments.

---

### Convert PDF

**Q: How to convert PDF to Word?**
A: Upload your PDF, select "PDF to Word", and download the editable .docx file. Our AI preserves formatting, tables, and images.

**Q: Will my formatting be preserved?**
A: Yes, our advanced conversion engine maintains original formatting including fonts, colors, images, and table structures.

**Q: Can I convert scanned PDFs to Word?**
A: Yes, but first use our OCR feature to extract text from the scanned image, then convert.
