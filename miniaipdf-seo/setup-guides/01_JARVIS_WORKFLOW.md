# Setup Guide: Jarvis Workflow Activation

**Integration**: Jarvis Mission Control  
**Time**: 5 minutes  
**Difficulty**: Easy  

---

## Step 1: Open Jarvis

1. Go to: https://jarvis-mission-control-seven.vercel.app
2. Login with your account

## Step 2: Find SEO Workflow

1. Click on **"Workflows"** in the sidebar
2. Look for: **"SEO Master Workflow"** or **"cmo4ri4ta0000simrn44sirgk"**
3. Click on it to open

## Step 3: Enable Trigger

1. Click **"Edit"** or **"Settings"**
2. Find **"Trigger"** section
3. Set to: **"Schedule"**
4. Schedule: **"0 9 * * *"** (09:00 PST daily)
5. Or use the visual scheduler to set: **Every day at 9:00 AM**

## Step 4: Connect Scripts

Once workflow is active, tell Playfish and I'll:
1. Upload the SEO scripts to the workflow
2. Connect Telegram notifications
3. Verify it's working

## Step 5: Test

1. Click **"Run Now"** to test manually
2. Check Telegram for the report
